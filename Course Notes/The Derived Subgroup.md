> [!info] **Definition: The Derived Subgroup**
> 
> Let $G$ be a group. The derived subgroup $[G,G]$ of $G$ is the subgroup generated by its commutators: $$ [G,G] = \langle x y x^{-1} y^{-1} \mid x, y \in G \rangle. $$
