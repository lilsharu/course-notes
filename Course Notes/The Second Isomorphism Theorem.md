> [!summary] **The Second Isomorphism Theorem**
> 
> Let $G$ be a group, $H$ be a subgroup of $G$, and $N$ be a normal subgroup of $G$. Then, $H N = \{h n : h \in H, n \in N\}$ is a subgroup of $G$, and $$ HN/N \simeq H/(H \cap N) $$
